About the Training Process

This will train a custom YOLOv5 model specifically for license plate detection using your dataset:

  1. Prepares your license plate images for training
  2. Formats the data in YOLO-compatible format
  3. Trains a model starting from YOLOv5 weights
  4. Saves the best model based on validation performance

After training, the model will be automatically available for detection in the main app.
